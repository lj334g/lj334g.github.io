Meet Aleta & Winter!

Aleta Heard, MSISM, CSPO

Chief Mission Officer - Founder

She has been recognized as a Woman of Excellence by the Pittsburgh Courier, 30 under 30 by Pittsburgh Business Times, and most recently, nominated for the 2022 PA Bankers Tomorrow’s Promise Award. Winter Clay, RN

Chief Operating Officer - Co-Founder

Winter Clay is an ambassador for Christ. An advocate for healthy families. Wife. Mother. Nurse. Doula and Midwife Apprentice. FAQ: Why Choose Maternal Care Services?
Maternal care services offer comprehensive healthcare for expectant mothers. They provide prenatal care, childbirth support, and postnatal care, ensuring a healthy journey for both mother and child.
FAQ: What Are the Benefits of Prenatal Checkups?
Prenatal checkups are crucial for monitoring the health of the mother and baby. They help detect and address any potential issues early, ensuring a safe and smooth pregnancy.
FAQ: What Should You Expect During Childbirth?
Childbirth is a unique experience for every mother. Maternal care teams provide support, pain management, and guidance to make the childbirth process as comfortable as possible.
