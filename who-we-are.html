Meet the Founders!

Aleta Heard, MSISM, CSPO

Chief Mission Officer - Founder

Aleta is an award-winning leader in the community and in her technical profession. She has been recognized as a Woman of Excellence by the Pittsburgh Courier, 30 under 30 by Pittsburgh Business Times, and most recently, nominated for the 2022 PA Bankers Tomorrow’s Promise Award.

She is an Innovative Strategist, Tech Expert, D&I Champion, Product Owner, Web Designer, Public Speaker, VP Of the PA Doula Commission, and a Doula herself.

Winter Clay, RN

Chief Operating Officer - Co-Founder

Winter Clay is an ambassador for Christ. An advocate for healthy families. Wife. Mother. Nurse. Doula and Midwife Apprentice.

She is the co-founder of Masters of Maternity, PA’s very first doula directory, passionately created as a multifaceted solution to combat the rising maternal mortality rate.
